Goal 7: Affordable and Clean Energy

Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all

Between 2000 and 2018, the number of people with electricity increased from 78% to 90%, and the number without electricity dipped to 789 million. Yet as the population continues to grow, so will the demand for affordable energy. An economy reliant on fossil fuels is creating drastic changes to our climate.

Investing in solar, wind, and thermal power, improving energy productivity, and ensuring access to clean energy for all is vital to achieving SDG 7 by 2030. Expanding infrastructure and upgrading technology to provide clean and efficient energy in all countries will not only encourage economic growth but also help the environment.

Key Statistics

90% with Electricity

The global percentage of people with access to electricity reached 90% by 2018.

789 Million Without Electricity

Approximately 789 million people still lack access to electricity.

How You Can Help

Educate Others
