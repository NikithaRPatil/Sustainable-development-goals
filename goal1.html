Goal 1: No Poverty

End poverty in all its forms everywhere

Poverty is one of the biggest challenges humanity faces. Despite significant progress in reducing extreme poverty, hundreds of millions of people still live below the poverty line, particularly in developing countries.

Ensuring that everyone has access to basic needs, resources, and opportunities is crucial to ending poverty and promoting sustainable development.

How You Can Help

Engage with local governments and organizations to advocate for policies that target poverty eradication and provide equal opportunities for all.

Support Charitable Organizations

Donate to or volunteer with charities and nonprofits working to provide resources, education, and opportunities for the impoverished.
